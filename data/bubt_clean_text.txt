BUBT Evaluation System:

For General Programs (Total: 100 marks):
  - Attendance: 5%
  - Quizzes: 5%
  - Class Tests & Tutorial: 10%
  - Assignment/Presentation/Lab/Project: 10%
  - Mid-Term Exam. (Middle of the Semester): 30%
  - Final Exam. (End of the Semester): 40%

For Llb Llm Programs (Total: 100 marks):
  - Attendance: 10%
  - Class Test: 10%
  - Assignment and Presentation: 10%
  - Mid term Examination (Middle of the Semester): 30%
  - Final Examination (End of the Semester): 40%


BUBT Grading System:

80% and above corresponds to letter grade A+ with a grade point of 4.0.
75% to less than 80% corresponds to letter grade A with a grade point of 3.75.
70% to less than 75% corresponds to letter grade A- with a grade point of 3.5.
65% to less than 70% corresponds to letter grade B+ with a grade point of 3.25.
60% to less than 65% corresponds to letter grade B with a grade point of 3.0.
55% to less than 60% corresponds to letter grade B- with a grade point of 2.75.
50% to less than 55% corresponds to letter grade C+ with a grade point of 2.5.
45% to less than 50% corresponds to letter grade C with a grade point of 2.25.
40% to less than 45% corresponds to letter grade D with a grade point of 2.0.
Less than 40% corresponds to letter grade F with a grade point of 0.0.

BUBT GPA Computation Example:

BUBT calculates GPA using the formula: GPA = Total (Grade Point x Credit Hours) / Total Credit Hours.

Example calculation with 5 courses:
Course 1 earned grade A+ with grade point 4.0 and 3 credit hours giving 12.0 points.
Course 2 earned grade B with grade point 3.0 and 3 credit hours giving 9.0 points.
Course 3 earned grade A with grade point 3.75 and 3 credit hours giving 11.25 points.
Course 4 earned grade B+ with grade point 3.25 and 2 credit hours giving 6.5 points.
Course 5 earned grade A- with grade point 3.5 and 1 credit hour giving 3.5 points.
Total credit hours: 12. Total grade points: 42.25. Final GPA: 42.25 / 12 = 3.52.

BUBT Academic Policies:

General Policies:
Caution money or Security money is Tk. 3,000 for Undergraduate Programs and Tk. 2,000 for Graduate Programs and is fully refundable.
Other Activity Fee of Tk. 3,000 is non-refundable and must be paid with 1st semester fees.
Other semester costs depend on the number of credits offered.
The university reserves the right to revise the fee structure on a semester basis.
No refund will be provided in case of admission cancellation.
The fee structure remains the same unless there is a course retake, grade improvement, or extension of study period.
Students must maintain a minimum CGPA of 2.00 for Undergraduate Programs and 2.50 for Graduate Programs to receive the university certificate.

F Grade Policy:
Students who receive an F grade must repeat the course within the next 3 consecutive semesters.
Full fee is charged for retaking the course.

Grade Improvement Policy:
Students can apply for grade improvement within 2 weeks after publication of results.
Fee for grade improvement is half the tuition fee calculated on a credit hour basis.
The improved grade is computed in the final transcript and the previous grade is marked with R which has no effect on GPA or CGPA.

BUBT Admission Requirements:

GPA: Minimum 2.50 in both SSC and HSC, or a combined GPA of 6.00 if one is 2.00
O and A Levels: At least 5 subjects in O Level and 2 in A Level. 4 subjects with B grade and 3 with C grade required. No D & E grade will be considered
Science Background: Math, Physics and Chemistry for CSE, DSE, EEE, Textile and Civil Engineering
GED Diploma: Not eligible for admission
Bachelor's Degree: Minimum CGPA of 2.00 with relevant field from a recognized university
Experience: Minimum 2 years of work experience for EMBA Program
Foreign Degree: Must submit equivalence certificate from MOE/UGC, Bangladesh

Documents Required for BUBT Admission:

One photocopy each of SSC/O-Level, HSC/A-Level/Diploma Certificates and Marksheets (Bachelor's Certificate and Marksheet for Master's Admission)
SSC and HSC Admit Cards, Registration Cards/Statement of Entry (for English Medium)
NID/Birth Certificate
Two passport size recent color photographs (studio lab print with formal for ID card)
Applicant/Candidate must be present at the time of admission

BUBT Scholarships and Waivers:

Merit Based Waivers at BUBT are awarded based on SSC and HSC GPA or CGPA results:
Students with GPA 10.00 or CGPA 4.00 receive 100% tuition waiver.
Students with GPA 9.50 to 9.99 or CGPA 3.91 to 3.99 receive 50% tuition waiver.
Students with GPA 9.00 to 9.49 or CGPA 3.80 to 3.90 receive 25% tuition waiver.

Special Waivers at BUBT are available for the following categories:
Poor and Meritorious students receive 10% to 100% waiver based on assessment.
Siblings of current BUBT students receive 25% waiver.
Sons or Daughters of Freedom Fighters receive 100% tuition waiver.
Students from Mirpur Area Colleges receive 10% waiver.
BUBT or DCC Graduates receive 20% waiver.
Students from Dhaka Commerce College receive 25% waiver.
Students from Principal Kazi Faruky College receive 25% waiver.

Bangladesh National Cadet Corps (BNCC) at BUBT is a para-military tri-services organization consisting of the Bangladesh Army, Navy, and Air Force. BUBT has been operating a BNCC Platoon since 2019 under the Ramna Regiment. The motto of BNCC is "Knowledge & Discipline". BNCC trains cadets to develop leadership, discipline, self-confidence and good human qualities. Activities include Battalion Camping, Regiment Camping, Central Camping, Victory Day Parade, Independence Day Parade, and Youth Exchange Programs with India, Nepal, Maldives and Singapore.
